Lightning Presentations

Patrick J. Burns, Moderator
ISAW/NYU
11:30am-12:30pm

In the interest of bringing attention to as many projects in Digital Classics, Archaeology, and related fields as possible, Ancient MakerSpaces 2018 will host an hour-long Lightning Presentations session. Presenters will have three minutes each to introduce their project, address its application in Classics research, pedagogy, or outreach, and explain how contributors, volunteers, or users can get involved. The schedule for the Lightning Presentations will be available soon.
